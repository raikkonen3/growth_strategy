Problem & Opportunity

This strategy's unit of analysis is the new user's journey combined with structural constraints unique to SWAG's business model. Search, recommendations, and UI/UX matter and are ongoing general optimizations; the three stages below each map to a problem that only exists because of SWAG's model. SWAG's own data shows 1-on-1 private chat has an 18x higher gifting rate than public livestreams, so the platform's real value sits in live and 1-on-1 interaction, which is why this strategy focuses there. 這份策略的分析單位,是新用戶旅程加上 SWAG 商業模式獨有的結構限制。Search、推薦、UI/UX 都很重要,是持續進行中的常態優化;以下三個環節,分別對應到只有 SWAG 這種模式才會出現的問題。SWAG 的數據顯示,1 對 1 私聊的送禮率是公開直播的 18 倍,平台真正的價值核心集中在直播和 1 對 1 互動,這也是這份策略聚焦在這裡的原因。

Opportunity:機會: If the three stages formed a self-reinforcing loop, smoother arrival and connection would create more positive interactions. Some users become repeat, active contributors, and their positive experience lowers the cost of the next round, so growth wouldn't need to restart from scratch each cycle. 若三個環節形成自我強化的循環,更順暢的抵達與連結會創造更多正向互動。部分用戶會成為持續回訪的活躍貢獻者,他們的正向體驗也會降低下一輪的成本,成長不需要每一輪都從零開始。

Goal:目標: Within 6 months, raise the rate and quality of new users completing Activation, and begin converting a portion into repeat, active contributors. 6 個月內,提升新用戶完成 Activation 的比例與品質,並開始把一部分用戶轉化為持續回訪的活躍貢獻者。
Scope: excludes long-term retention once users are established (spend depth, subscriptions, scaled referral) 範疇:不包含用戶穩定後的長期經營(消費深度、訂閱、規模化推薦)

01

Arrival抵達 Arrival

Discovering and following a creator發現並開始關注一個創作者
Two-Sided Marketplace Imbalance雙邊市場失衡
New users don't know what to do after landing, or where to find a creator of interest. Live and 1-on-1 are a creator's real-time service with limited capacity; when traffic concentrates on top creators, it hurts both sides, quality drops for users piling onto the same creator, and new creators can't get enough traffic to survive. This affects whether the platform keeps attracting new creators. 新用戶進站後不知道該做什麼,也不知道去哪裡找感興趣的創作者。直播和 1 對 1 是創作者的即時服務,產能有限;流量集中頭部,會同時傷害兩邊,用戶這邊互動品質下降,新進創作者那邊分不到流量、難以生存。這關係到平台能不能持續吸引新創作者。

02

Connection連結 Connection

Deciding to take the first step, the key decision point before Activation決定是否跨出第一步,即 Activation 前的關鍵決策點
Diamond Economy鑽石經濟
Users may hesitate from lacking diamonds or lacking information (pricing, response speed, activity level). This friction is specific to SWAG: no subscription, every interaction costs diamonds. Arrival is the precondition for Connection. 用戶可能因為缺乏鑽石,或缺乏資訊(定價、回覆速度、活躍度)而猶豫。這個卡點是 SWAG 特有的鑽石經濟,沒有訂閱制,每次互動都要付費。抵達是連結的前提。

03

Amplification擴散 Amplification

Whether a positive interaction sustains itself into becoming an active contributor正向互動是否能延續,進而成為活躍貢獻者
Privacy Concern隱私顧慮
The platform has no mechanism to carry a positive interaction forward. Connection's success means real earnings for creators, who typically already have external followings and are more willing to bring those fans onto the platform. This strategy chose creator-driven referral over user-invites-user, since adult-platform users don't want their social circle to know they use it. Creators are already public-facing, so this concern doesn't apply to them. 平台目前沒有機制讓正向互動延續下去。連結成功對創作者是實際收益,創作者本身通常已有外部粉絲,更願意帶他們進平台。這裡選擇創作者驅動而非用戶邀請用戶,因為成人平台用戶不想讓社交圈知道自己在用,創作者公開露臉,沒有這層顧慮。
Why it matters:為什麼重要: These three stages are interdependent. Arrival's output is the precondition for Connection; Connection's output is the motivation behind Amplification. A gap in any one limits the payoff from the other two. 三個環節環環相扣。抵達的產出是連結的前提;連結的產出是擴散的動機來源。任一個缺口都會限制其他兩者的效益。

General validation rule通用驗證原則
When multiple variables are tested within a bet, evaluate for diminishing returns before combining effective ones. If only one works, roll it out alone to avoid unnecessary complexity. 當一個 bet 內同時測試多個變數時,先評估疊加是否有邊際效益遞減,再決定是否合併上線;若只有一項有效,就單獨上線,避免不必要的複雜度。
